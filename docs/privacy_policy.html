Data We Process

  • Message content: used to generate auto‑replies and maintain lightweight memory. Stored locally in dayle_data/ on your server.

  • Telemetry: minimal logs; optional counters at /metrics (no PII).

Purpose

Provide respectful, concise auto‑replies; de‑escalate tense threads; propose next steps; and keep brief context.

Sharing

No data is sold. Optional integrations (Twilio/Messenger) transmit message content only if you enable them, under their terms.

Security

  • HTTPS recommended for all endpoints
  • License data encrypted at rest
  • Time‑bounded HTTP requests; signature verification for Messenger

Retention

Conversation memory persists until you delete it. You can purge per contact via the app or API.

Your Controls

  • Configure banned words and preferred phrases
  • Disable automation per contact
